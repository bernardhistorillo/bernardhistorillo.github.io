This groundbreaking study offers an innovative solution for individuals suffering from pulmonary diseases. This expert system has been designed to provide users with a comprehensive list of prescription and alternative medicines, tailored to their specific health condition. With six interactive modules, the system is user-friendly and provides a wealth of information on dosage, content, and combination, as well as age restrictions, administration, indications, contraindications, warnings, and precautions.

The Medicine Information Module offers a wide range of brand and generic drugs, along with vital information about each drug. Math Dommable

About Math Dommable

An exciting Android 2D app game that blends the fun and strategic elements of both domino and scrabble games - a perfect combination for puzzle lovers and math enthusiasts alike. The objective of the game is to create mathematical expressions using dominoes and earn points based on the results of each expression. With a visually engaging game board, players can immerse themselves in the game while improving their math skills.

As players progress through the game, they will face increasing challenges that require strategic planning and problem-solving skills to succeed. The game offers different difficulty levels, including easy, average, and difficult, allowing players of all skill levels to enjoy the game at their own pace. The game also offers a 'player versus computer' feature, making it possible to play against the computer and test your skills.

With the game ending once all dominoes have been used up or no more valid moves can be made, every turn counts, and players must think strategically to outscore their opponents. The player who earns the highest score emerges victorious, making for an exhilarating gameplay experience.

Overall, this app is a must-have for anyone who loves puzzle games, math, or strategic gameplay.
